The Core Platform

REAP —
Real Estate Acquisition Protocol

The question every investor asks on every deal is the same: is this a good deal or not? REAP exists to answer that question with institutional precision — in minutes, not days.

Deal Underwriting

Input purchase price, rents, expenses, and financing. REAP outputs cash-on-cash return, cap rate, IRR, equity multiple, and a composite deal score — instantly.

Risk Analysis

Every deal gets a risk index score flagging where the numbers are fragile — vacancy sensitivity, debt coverage, exit assumptions — so you know exactly where the deal can break.

Performance Tracking

After closing, REAP tracks actual operational results against original underwriting projections — creating accountability between what was promised and what's being delivered.

Standalone or Integrated

REAP works as a fully independent SaaS platform for any investor anywhere — or as the operational backbone across the entire Suarez Global ecosystem.
