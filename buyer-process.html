The Process
Seven Steps.
One Clear Path.

From the moment you raise your hand to the moment you get the keys, here is exactly how we work together.

01

Initial Inquiry & Lead Capture

It all starts here. You reach out — whether through our website, a referral, or a conversation — and we capture the essentials so we can begin building your plan.

What Happens

  • You visit our designated intake page and submit your information
  • We capture your name, contact details, and general buying interest
  • We confirm receipt and set expectations for the next conversation
02

Buyer Information Checklist

We gather a comprehensive set of details to paint your full financial picture. These are the same questions any lender will ask — by collecting them early, we position you from strength instead of scrambling later.

Personal & Household

  • Full names, household size, current living situation
  • Monthly rent or mortgage and lease expiration date
  • Household structure and family goals

Financial Snapshot

  • All debts with balances, rates, and lenders
  • Credit scores for buyer and spouse
  • Assets: savings, investments, business ownership, insurance

Income & Employment

  • W-2 or self-employed status; length at current job
  • Household income including supplemental sources
  • Any upcoming job transitions (critical for loan timing)

Goals & Vision

  • End-game financial and life goals
  • Property preferences and buying timeline
  • First-time buyer status and risk tolerance
03

Initial Buyer Consultation

This is a deep-dive coaching session — in person or virtual. We start with the end in mind: what does your life look like when you've achieved your goals? Then we work backwards from there to build the plan.

Vision & Rapport

We ask what success looks like for you and your family — retirement, legacy, freedom, security. Your goals become the anchor for every recommendation that follows.

Financial Deep-Dive

We walk through every number together. Most buyers discover they're in a stronger position than they thought. We identify strengths, flag obstacles, and frame everything as solvable.

Education & Concepts

For first-time or newer buyers, we cover the fundamentals: property types, house hacking, equity building, productive debt, FHA programs, and down payment assistance.

Action Items

You leave with clear next steps: pull credit reports, set up an LLC if applicable, access our property research tools, and prepare for lender introductions.

04

Market Analysis & Data Review

We analyze the market like operators — not like agents pulling comps. You'll understand exactly what's happening in your target area so you can make informed decisions, not emotional ones.

What We Analyze

  • Buyer's market vs. seller's market conditions
  • Days on market and absorption rate
  • Price per sq ft: active, sold, and under contract
  • Listing price vs. closing price equilibrium

Macro Factors

  • Interest rate trends and refinance timing
  • New construction and zoning developments
  • Government incentive programs
  • Seasonal and local economic shifts
05

Goal Setting & Strategy Development

With market data in hand, we align your goals with a realistic, strategic approach. We treat this like a scientific method — form a hypothesis, test it through showings and offers, and adjust based on results.

Strategy Tracks

  • First-Time Buyer: FHA loan, down payment assistance, house hack potential
  • Investment-Focused: Hard money, renovate, build equity, refinance
  • Generational Wealth: Multi-unit, family compound, trust structuring

Setting Expectations

How many homes to view, how many offers to expect before acceptance (plan for around 10), typical timelines from search to close, and what a competitive offer looks like in the current market.

06

Lender Connection & Pre-Approval

We don't just hand you a lender's phone number. We prepare a financial summary from your checklist and send it ahead — so when you call, they already know who you are and what you're working with.

How It Works

  • We prepare your buyer financial summary for the lender
  • You come in as a warm referral, not a cold application
  • Traditional lenders for strong W-2 profiles
  • Private and hard-money lenders for investment plays

Why Timing Matters

If you're planning a job change, we get the loan application submitted while you still show stable employment. A pre-approved buyer is a stronger buyer — this step prevents wasted time on properties outside your realistic budget.

07

Property Search & Offers

With your criteria, market insights, strategy, and financing in place — we go live. Automated MLS alerts, AI property tools, scheduled showings, and strategic offers. This is where the plan becomes real.

Search & Showings

  • Automated MLS search tailored to your criteria
  • AI property research tool for self-evaluation
  • Exposure visits to build your eye and confidence
  • Comparative market analysis on every property of interest

Offers & Negotiation

  • Offers drafted in alignment with your strategy
  • Full negotiation on your behalf — terms, price, contingencies
  • Regular check-ins and strategy adjustments
  • All documents organized in your dedicated client folder
